Fix: Invitations Not Visible to Invitees

Adds status:'pending' & token to invitation insert, auto-refresh on tab focus

Cause: inviteToWorkspace() inserts without status:'pending' or token. The getMyInvitations() query filters by status = 'pending', so NULL-status rows are invisible to invitees.

Fix: Add status:'pending' and token: crypto.randomUUID() to the insert in supabase.js. Add visibility-change auto-refresh and case-insensitive duplicate check in App.jsx.
